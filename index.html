Purpose of the Website

Study Support Hub is designed for junior and senior high school students who experience difficulties in studying. The website helps students identify common academic problems such as poor time management, lack of focus, difficulty understanding lessons, reading comprehension problems, and low motivation.

By selecting their learning problem, students receive practical study strategies, tools, and learning resources that help improve their study habits, organization, focus, and academic performance. The website promotes independent and organized learning in a simple and accessible way.
