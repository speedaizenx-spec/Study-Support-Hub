About FindIt

Our mission to help students recover lost belongings

Problem Explanation

Students often lose their belongings in school, such as textbooks, water bottles, jackets, and electronic devices. Traditionally, there has been no organized digital system to track and recover these items, leading to permanent loss and frustration.

Purpose of the System

FindIt was created to provide a professional digital lost-and-found solution specifically designed for school environments. Our platform bridges the gap between lost items and their owners through a centralized, searchable database.

Target Users

Students

All grade levels from 6 to 12

Teachers

School faculty and staff

School Staff

Administration and support staff

How the System Helps Students

Faster Recovery

Helps students recover lost items faster by connecting them with finders immediately.

Reduces Confusion

Eliminates the chaos of traditional lost-and-found areas with organized digital tracking.

Improves Responsibility

Encourages students to be more responsible with their belongings through the reporting system.

Enhances School Organization

Creates a more organized and efficient school environment for everyone.
